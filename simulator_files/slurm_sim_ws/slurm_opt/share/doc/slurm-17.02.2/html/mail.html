Mailing Lists

We maintain two Slurm mailing lists:

  • slurm-announce@schedmd.com is designated for communications about Slurm releases. The ability to post to this list is restricted to a few Slurm developers [low traffic].
  • slurm-dev@schedmd.com is designated for communications to Slurm developers and is typically used by Slurm users or system administrators. Any person subscribed to this mailing list may post to it. [high traffic at times].

The slurm-dev@schedmd.com mailing list is a good mechanism for the user community to share information.

You can join either list by subscribing here.

You can unsubscribe by logging into your mailing list profile, or email unsubscribe@schedmd.com.

The archive of the slurm-dev list is online:
http://dir.gmane.org/gmane.comp.distributed.slurm.devel
and
http://groups.google.com/group/slurm-devel
